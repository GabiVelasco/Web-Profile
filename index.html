THE INTEGRATION OF DANCE AND TECHNOLOGY


With a unique combination of dance and technology, Gabriela brings a creative perspective to the world of computer science. Through her experiences as a dancer and choreographer, she integrates artistic forms of expression into her technological projects.

This creative approach allows her to develop innovative solutions and create unique perspectives at the ever-evolving intersection of art and technology.
